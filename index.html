CFD Templates

Use this section to get instant links for CFD templates for all scenarios and radiator/fan configurations

V11

The standard go-to CFD template, without a radiator region.

V11R

CFD template with a porous region for the radiator.

V11X

Advanced CFD template using URANS turbulence model.

V11 (Pitch)

Standard pitch template, with pitch angle of 1.8 deg

V11R (Pitch)

Pitch template with radiators. Currently unavailable

V11 (Crosswind)

Crosswind template for people running without radiators.

V11R (Crosswind)

Crosswind template with a radiator region, 10 deg yaw.

Advanced template for simulating yaw, rotating the car around a circular domain.
